2nd MSNE Retreat

2018-06-01 · 77 · 1
events

The Spring 2018 MSNE Retreat took place in Ofterschwang, Allgäu from May 31 to June 3.

Apart from thirty participating first and second year MSNE Students, we welcomed the following speakers for invited talks:

  • Thursday: Prof. Dr. Jakob Macke, Macke Lab Lab, Technical University of Munich.
  • Friday: Prof. Dr. Benjamin Grewe, Institute of Neuroinformatics, ETH Zurich.
  • Saturday: Prof. Dr. Thomas Euler, Institute for Opthalmic Research, University of Tübingen.

A detailed post about the retreat will follow soon!
